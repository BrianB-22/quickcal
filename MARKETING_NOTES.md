# MacMenuBar.com Submission

**App Name:** QuickCal

**Tagline:**
Calendar, world clock, and time assistant for global teams — all in your menu bar.

**Short Description:**
QuickCal puts a full calendar, world clock, and natural-language time assistant one click away in your menu bar. Built for professionals working across time zones — see holidays in 16 countries, pin any timezone to your menu bar, and ask plain-English questions like "how many business days until end of quarter" or "what time is it in Singapore." No internet required. No subscriptions.

**Category:** Productivity / Utilities

**Key Features to highlight:**
- 16-country holiday calendar with color-coded dots
- World clock with flags, drag-to-reorder, and menu bar pin
- Natural language Q&A — date math, time zones, business days, countdowns
- Fully offline — no accounts, no API keys, no subscriptions
